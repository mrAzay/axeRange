About us
An axe is an instrument of hunting, cutting down trees, war and self-affirmation. For the sheer joy of competition and to keep their skills sharp, throwing competitions became a way to showcase talents and sharpen them.
What is axe throwing like? It is similar to throwing knives, target shooting and darts. The goal being, to hit your target.
Throwing an axe develops agility, accuracy and a sense of balance. It helps to let off steam and gain self-confidence. Spending time in the axe range will let you to de-stress and feel like a real warrior.
It’s hard to find a more legitimate way to release adrenaline and free your primal instincts.
Come and throw!
how it works
How to begin axe throwing? Take the axe in your hand. Close your eyes, take a deep breath and count to three. Before aiming, DON’T forget to open your eyes! Aim and through that axe!
Each movement will make the heartbeat faster and fill the blood with adrenaline. An incomparable feeling - to see how an axe thrown by you hits the target.
Just try it and you will challenge your skills and senses and make lasting, fun memories with family and friends.
Axe throwing is a game that gives us an invaluable resource to be ourselves.
Leave your routine and come have fun with us!

The World Axe Throwing league
The World Axe Throwing League is not here to promote a single brand or image. WATL is here to promote the sport of indoor axe throwing as a whole while bringing together all indoor urban axe throwing clubs from around the world. We seek to standardize the sport of axe throwing - from official league rules, the points tracking system, safety protocols, axe throwing techniques, and everything in between. The prime directive of WATL this year is to achieve the unprecedented; to host the first-ever worldwide axe throwing tournament!
